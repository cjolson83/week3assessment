Country Club

Country Club Historic District is located in Omaha, Nebraska from 50th to 56th Streets, from Corby to Seward Streets. With dozens of homes built between 1925 and 1949 in the late 19th and 20th Century Revival styles, the community was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2004.
